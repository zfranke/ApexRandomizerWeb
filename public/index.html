Apex Legends Randomizer

Randomize a legend and a starting weapon. The legend you get is what you pick in the legend screen and the weapon you pick is what you have to grab first when you drop into the map. If you do not have the legend you rolled, you get to reroll.

Modes:

  • Easy Mode: Randomize as much as you like until you get a combo you like.
  • Medium: Can only pick once. Reroll if weapon is care package weapon.
  • Hard Mode: Can only pick once, no rerolls (unless legend based).
